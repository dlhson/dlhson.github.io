Only your name is enough. That's it! If you want to add another few lines about you, that'd be great. I'll get back to you within 24 hours. I promise!
Now, this is my assumptions about you: I guess, just like the rest of us, you are also thrilled by the idea of freedom in thinking and action, financial independence, legacy, or simply personal fulfillment. You just don't want to go alone at the beginning.
Lausanne Startup Book Club is started in March 2015 with one purpose: to help you and me learn and share all things related to building and scaling a startup. The initial plan is as follows. We meet up once per month. We read books and articles of all topics that have to do with startup. After knowing more about our common interests, we will see how the club can evolve.
Startup is challenging but shouldn't be a lonely journey. With the club, we learn and grow together.
What do you say?
